>> Ethical & Compliance Check:

1. Ensure that all negotiations are conducted in compliance with applicable laws and industry standards.

2. Maintain a high level of integrity in all negotiations and interactions, while adhering to ethical standards of conduct.

3. Familiarize yourself with the relevant regulations and industry best practices.

4. Be aware of conflicts of interests and potential ethical violations.

5. Seek legal advice when in doubt.

6. Never offer or accept any form of bribery or inducement.

7. Ensure that all agreements are in writing and signed by all parties.

8. Document all negotiations to ensure accuracy and transparency.

9. Abide by the terms of any agreement reached.
